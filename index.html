I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Dakuo Wang in the Human-Centered AI Lab (NEU HAI). My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My work spans co-designing AI-driven clinical decision support systems for remote care, and exploring how technologies, such as AI, can improve patient-provider communication amid the time-pressured emergency department and strengthen multi-stakeholders (physicians, nurses, patients, and caregivers) collaboration in the post-surgery care.
